    Deployment Options

    SmartPAL can be deployed in two different modes, depending on your course goals and preferences:

    • As an Instructional Tool â€“ Use SmartPAL as a teaching and learning support tool
    • As Research Participation â€“ Use SmartPAL as part of an approved research study on learning technologies

    Option 1: Deploy SmartPAL as Instructional Tool

    The Instructional Tool mode allows you to use SmartPAL as a teaching and learning support tool, similar to other instructional technologies.

    Key characteristics

    • No IRB involvement
    • No required surveys
    • Participation credit optional
    • Minimal instructor overhead

    Best suited for

    • First-time SmartPAL users
    • Courses where participation credit or research framing is not desirable

    Data use

    • Aggregate, de-identified usage data used for platform improvement
    • Not used for human subjects research

    Option 2: Deploy SmartPAL as Research Participation

    The Research Participation mode allows SmartPAL to be used as part of an IRB-approved study on learning technologies.

    Key characteristics

    • Covered by IRB approval (student consent exempt)
    • Voluntary participation by students
    • Background and post-course surveys
    • Participation credit required (e.g., 1-2% extra credit or course credit)
    • Low instructor overhead

    Best suited for

    • Instructors interested in contributing to learning research
    • Instructors comfortable offering participation incentives

    Incentives

    • A small amount of participation incentives is required to compensate students for the additional time and sustained effort required

    Data use

    • De-identified data used for IRB-approved research
    • Aggregate, de-identified usage data used for platform improvement
